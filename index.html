Wordpress

After being launched in 2003, Wordpress has become the most popular CMS (Content Management System) on the market today. As of 2017, over 27% of websites are built using Wordpress and over 50,000 websites are made daily using Wordpress. Being a very flexible platform, I can build anything from an eCommerce website to a Blog. In addition to being a`ble to create any type of webpage, it is 99% SEO (Search Engine Optimization) friendly!

Responsive Design

Having a responsive website, meaning that your webpage can be viewed on any size screen, is necessary for a successful business. In fact, a recent study concluded that over 80% of users research a business online after it is recommended to them. If the website is not a responsive design, their opinion will be drastically lowered and likely to use your competitor.

Web Design

Designing a webpage is much more than what content is written. Everything from color schemes, fonts, pictures and SEO all come in to play. Web Design is an excellent way to create a user-friendly webpage. Did you know that humans communicate easier using pictures and colors than reading? Creating a clean-coded webpage allows search engines to read your webpage pushing YOUR website to the top of search engines. Therefore, you will be found before your competitors.
